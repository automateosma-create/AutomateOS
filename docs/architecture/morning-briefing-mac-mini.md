# Morning Briefing and Mac mini

- **Purpose:** Give Maaz a low-friction daily overview of schedule, priorities, deadlines, health context, and system status.
- **Delivery:** Generated automatically each morning and shown through the Mac mini desktop app; the same underlying brief may also appear in GPT, email, or future mobile surfaces.
- **Inputs:** Calendar, Tasks, Daily_Briefs, recent actuals, relevant email-derived items, health data, and workflow status.
- **Content:** Today’s fixed events, realistic priorities, urgent items, notable changes, and a concise AutomateOS health check.
- **Mac mini role:** Persistent ambient display and desktop access point, not a separate source of truth.
- **Rules:** Reuse shared data, avoid duplicate entry, keep the brief concise, and surface only actionable exceptions.
- **Health check:** Confirm the brief generated on time, required data sources were reachable, and the desktop app received the latest version; failures are logged and surfaced clearly.
